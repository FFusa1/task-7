New generation ceramic tiles

Next year will see the return of naturalness: texture of wood, natural stone or marble, even concrete, in 2023 our love for ceramic tiles (realistically) imitating nature will have no equal. But it's not enough: colours will play a big role in 2023 trends and white is on top of the list. While we love to keep you informed on what's hot in the tiles and wood world, a reflection is also needed. Coming back from CERSAIE2022, we could not help but notice that there has been somehow a slowdown in creativity: is this due to Covid? Is it because of the war? Certainly both agents play a big role in how we perceive life. But how have artists been affected by these troubled times? Fear and uncertainty seem to have taken over creativity.
